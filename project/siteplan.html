A planning document for the **Armor Gate** e-commerce website, specializing in medieval armor and weaponry.

Site Name

The site or domain name is the primary identifier for branding and recognition. It must be relevant to the project.

Site Name: **Armor Gate**

Description: This name was chosen because "Armor" is the core product, and "Gate" suggests a portal or entry point to a collection of high-quality items, evoking the feeling of entering a secure, historical armory or keep. It is short, memorable, and directly relates to the project's focus.

Optional Domain: armorgate.com

Site Purpose

Describes the site's scope and the services/information provided to the user.

The site serves as a premium **e-commerce platform** for the sale of authentic, handcrafted medieval armor and historical weaponry. It will provide **detailed product descriptions**, **high-quality imagery**, and a **secure checkout process**. Additionally, it will offer educational content regarding the history and craftsmanship of the armor pieces to build customer trust and engagement.

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

Questions asked by a target audience visitor, which drive the site's content and design.

1. **"I need a full suit of 15th-century Gothic plate armor for an upcoming historical reenactment. Do you offer custom-sizing, and what is the typical lead time?"** (Drives the need for a **Custom Order/Sizing** page and **FAQ/Shipping** details.)

2. **"I'm new to collecting swords. What's the difference between a functional, battle-ready sword and a decorative piece, and how can I care for my purchase?"** (Drives the need for a **Product Guide/Comparison** section and **Care & Maintenance** information.)

Color Usage:

  • **Primary Color (#3f2212 - Dark Brown):** Used for **Headings (H1, H2, etc.)**, **Navigation Bar Background**, and key **Action Buttons**. Represents wood, leather, and historical quality.
  • **Accent Color (#a08040 - Muted Gold):** Used for **Link/Hover States**, **Borders/Dividers**, and **Special Pricing Text**. Represents ornate metalwork and a premium feel.
  • **Secondary Color (#e6e6e6 - Light Silver/Gray):** Used for the overall **Background Color of the Page** and specific **Product Card Backgrounds**. Represents metal/steel and creates a clean contrast.
  • **Text Color (#1a1a1a - Very Dark Gray):** Used for **Paragraph/Body Text** for excellent readability against the lighter backgrounds.

üìù Typography

Selected fonts and their applications across the site for consistency and tone.

Font 1: 'Times New Roman', serif (Used for Headings)

Application: All main **Headings (H1, H2, H3)**. Chosen for its traditional, strong, and authoritative appearance, evoking historical documents and quality craftsmanship.

Font 2: 'Georgia', serif (Used for Body Text and Paragraphs)

Application: All **Body Paragraphs, Lists, and General UI Text**. Chosen as a highly readable, elegant serif font that complements 'Times New Roman' while being optimized for screen reading.
